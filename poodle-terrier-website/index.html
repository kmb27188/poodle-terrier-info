History





Poodles originated in Germany and Terriers
orginated in England. Poodles were originally
bred as hunters, bringing birds back to their
owners and Terriers were originally bred to kill
vermin. Both Poodles and Terriers are active
dogs and are great around children. This makes
them great family pets.




Fun Facts!





There are a lot of interesting things to know about
poodle terriers. A few of them are, most of them
are hypoallergenic, they do not shed like most dogs
and they are not as popular as other mixes. A few
more are, they live thirteen to sixteen years, they
are loyal, loveable, and intelligent. One final fact
is they crave attention and will be near you all
day and night.
