Vet visits

You should take your dog to the vet yearly
for a check up and any time you notice
something unusual about them. I take my
dog to the Town and Country Veterinary
Clinic in Chesterfield, Va. There are many
vets that you can choose from depending
on where you live. Here are some of the
ones that are in Chesterfield, Va.
