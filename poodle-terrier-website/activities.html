Activities

It is important to exercise with your dog everyday and taking them on a walk around the neighborhood is the
best way to do it. Even taking them on a 15 to 20 minute walk a day will keep them fit and it will be beneficial
to you as well. Giving them plenty of toys to play with at home is a great way to bond with them. This will
lead to exercise inside the house, too as they will constantly bring you their toys to throw to them. You can
also take them to a dog park to play with other dogs but I would be careful about that right now since there
is a respiratory virus going around for dogs. Once we get past the winter months, dog parks will probably
be a better option.
