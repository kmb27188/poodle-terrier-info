Food

There are a lot of brands of dog food like Blue Buffalo, Purina One, IAMS, Pedigree, etc. I feed my dogs
Purina One dog food with a blend of real turkey and venison because it was recommended by my vet
and they love it. We fill up their bowls once in the morning with food and give them treats throughout the
day. Poodle Terriers are very good at pacing themselves with food throughout the day so there is no need
to feed a cup here and there. They do go through quite a bit of water though so we do need to fill those up
more than once a day. We also keep water upstairs and downstairs for them so they can have it
throughout the day and night.
